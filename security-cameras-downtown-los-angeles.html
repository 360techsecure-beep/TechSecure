Downtown LA is a dense mix of residential lofts, ground-floor retail, restaurants, and offices — which means security here is rarely a single-camera job. Businesses along Spring, Main, and the Arts District typically need overlapping coverage of entrances, registers, stockrooms, and alleys, plus footage they can pull up instantly from a phone or laptop. Loft and condo residents, meanwhile, want tight coverage of entryways, hallways, and parking without a tangle of visible wiring.

We build multi-camera systems with centralized recording, access control, and remote monitoring so you can keep an eye on a DTLA storefront from anywhere — whether you're across town or out of state. Every install is clean, discreet, and configured to give you reliable, high-resolution evidence if you ever need it.

What we install for Downtown LA properties

  • 4K HD cameras — indoor, outdoor, and 360° units with night vision and weatherproof builds.
  • Smart alerts & AI detection — person, package, and vehicle detection that skips false alarms.
  • Remote monitoring — live feeds and recordings on your phone from anywhere.
  • Video doorbell cameras — see and record every visitor and delivery at your door.
