Protecting a business is a different challenge than protecting a home. Storefronts, offices, restaurants, and warehouses across Los Angeles need overlapping camera coverage, reliable recording, and footage that stands up when there's a theft, a liability claim, or a dispute. Tech Secure 360 designs and installs commercial surveillance systems built around how your business actually operates.

We handle everything from a few cameras for a single retail location to multi-site networks with centralized recording and access control. Every system is configured for remote monitoring, so owners and managers can check live feeds and pull recordings from a phone or laptop — across town or across the country.

Commercial systems we install

  • Multi-camera surveillance — full coverage of entries, registers, stockrooms, parking, and blind spots.
  • Access control — keypad, fob, and app-based entry to control who gets in and when.
  • Remote & multi-site monitoring — manage every location from one dashboard.
  • High-capacity recording — reliable storage with footage retention sized to your needs.
  • AI detection — person and vehicle alerts that cut through false alarms.
