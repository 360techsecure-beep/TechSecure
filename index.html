FAQ

Questions, answered

Are there monthly fees? +

No. Our systems record locally to a DVR or NVR, so there are no required monthly recording fees. Optional cloud backup is available if you want it, but it's never required to keep your footage.

Can I watch my cameras from my phone? +

Yes. Every system we install includes remote viewing, so you can watch live and recorded footage from your phone anywhere. We set up the app and network for you.

My DVR or NVR stopped recording — can you fix it? +

Usually, yes. We repair and troubleshoot DVR and NVR systems, cameras that dropped offline, and recorders that quit recording — often without replacing the whole system.

Can you upgrade my old cameras to 4K? +

In many cases we can replace old analog or low-resolution cameras with HD or 4K and reuse your existing wiring, so you get a big quality jump without starting from scratch.

Do you serve both homes and businesses? +

Yes — homes, apartment buildings, retail stores, gas stations, warehouses, and offices across Greater Los Angeles.
